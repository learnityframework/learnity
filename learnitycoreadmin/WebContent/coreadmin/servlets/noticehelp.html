Where can I use LearnITy Notice?

You can use LearnITy Notice from anywhere in the world or organization with a browser and Internet/intranet access. LearnITy Notice will work with almost any browser, including Netscape and Internet Explorer versions 5.0 and higher.

LearnITy Notice Board is a part of Portal, which are displayed list of notices on the portal. The Notices are collection public event, which are submitted by users or admin till date. Its basic functionality is focus on the matter and all users of that concern will aware of their organization information. The users can also add a notice but they can delete/modify their own notices. The LearnITy Administrator can add and also modify / delete existing notices.

List of Notice on LearnITy Portal

On the Notice board, first five last posted notices will show. Later you can view next five clicking on the "next" button. After Clicking on the next button then automatically will be appeared "previous" button and if there is more notice then again next button will be appeared. Clicking on "Add new" button, then it will be appeared a new window.

How can view details of Notice?

Clicking on the particular notice from the above list of notices will be opened in new window with all information. The selected Notice will be displayed its heading, account with the name of the user who posted that particular notice, date of notice creation, notice description and also any attachment. There is also a provision for the user to add new notice, modify selected notice and delete selected notice and to view next/previous notice.

The screen has six buttons. Clicking on the "Next/Previous" button, it will be displayed next/ previous notice as par your selection of button. Whenever you will click on "Done" button then this window will be closed and Portal window will be refreshed.

How can add a new Notice?

Clicking the "Add" or Portal "Add new" button then it will be appeared a new window for a new notice. The user will be entered the all information in the below screen. When you are adding a notice then two fields must have to enter heading and notice body information otherwise your notice will not be accepted and displayed a valid information to the user. If you have more information then you can attach a file with notice. The attachment field is optional. The user name will be taken automatically which are already logged in and Notice Time will also be automatically recorded when your notice will be submitted to database then your notice will be added with list of notice and portal screen will be refreshed.

How do I attach a file with notice?

Click on the "Attach files" link then following screen will be appeared. Clicking on the browser button to locate the attachment file. After that you will click on "Attach files" the selected file will be attached and again click on the "Done" button finally file will be attached with your notice and this attachment window screen will be disappeared.

How can modify/delete a Notice?

The user can modify or delete only your own notice by first selecting a particular notice from the list of notices. Next select an existing notice from the notice list Click on the "Modify"button and change as per need in a selected notice. The Notice Heading will not be modified but you can be modified other information. Click on the "Submit" button then your submitted notice will be modified permanently in your database. Administrator can also modify or delete the user notice. Similarly you can delete a selected notice.
